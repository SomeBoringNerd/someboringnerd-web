Through The Dark

A game that I'm creating



What is it ?

Through the Dark is a game where you play as Diana, a 21 years old student that has seen many people of her town disappear, and the local police don't do anything about it. Her goal is to find what happened and put the criminals to a stop.


Inspired by

>Danganrompa for the look and feel
>Team Fortress 2 / Half Life for the movement system
>My friends for some characters
>Legend of zelda BOTW for some game mechanics (quest for exemple)
>Hitman for the stealth parts


Features

One day, I'll have to make a separate page for that ;')


Note book

In Diana's room, you can find a note-book where you can write anything in ! useful to keep track of what you are doing

Reputation Mechanic

In order to get informations, you can talk to students and befriend them. Because honestly, why would you tell anything to a strange girl that no one seem to know ?

Relationship

you can befriend some people a little bit too much and be lover with them. you can be in a relationship with 5 different characters (2 of them are already implemented)
